Flow Explanation

  1. RP website generates a deeplink to the PasskeyGuard app
  2. PasskeyGuard app opens the RP website in SFSafariViewController
  3. WebAuthn registration runs in the main app context (not the extension)
  4. DCAppAttest proves the app's hardware integrity
  5. Deeplink back to the app after successful registration
